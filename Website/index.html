MY TASTE IN MUSIC GENRES ACROSS TIME

This website was made for two reasons: to organzie all of the music I like using Timelines and Genres, but more importantly to learn how to make websites. We will cover everything from ABBA to Zed's Dead. We will explore classic rock, known for show stopping instrumentals as well as gangster rap which focuses more on thought provoking lyrics. Some songs have a deep and profound meaning, others are just fun to listen to. Without rambling on any further, let's dive right in!
